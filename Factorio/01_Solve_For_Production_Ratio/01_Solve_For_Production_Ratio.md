# Solve for Production Ratio of 三色板工厂

The Three-Color Chip Factory (or 三色板工厂) is a factory design of Chip Production.

It is consisted of four main components:

1) Copper Wire

2) Green Chip

3) Red Chip

4) Blue Chip



The intake of resources:

1) Iron Plate

2) Copper Plate

3) Plastic

4) Sulfuric Acid
